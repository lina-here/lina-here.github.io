Introduction

Na Li works at the intersection of intellectual property, international cooperation, knowledge sharing, and digital transformation. She has 5 years of experience in communication, outreach, digital transformation, and project management.

🥇 At the Intersection of Intellectual Property, Technology, and Global Development

With domain knowledge of intellectual property and digital transformation. Her work focuses on how innovation, knowledge sharing, and emerging technologies can support economic growth and sustainable development. She worked as a Young Expert from 2024 to 2026 at the World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO) and the International Telecommunication Union (ITU), contributing to global outreach initiatives, digital transformation projects, and knowledge-sharing platforms. Previously, she advised Chinese local governments and public services providers in China on smart cities and digital governance.

Na LI holds a Master of Public Policy from UCLA (USA, 2020) and a Bachelor's degree in Law and Political Science from Zhejiang University(China, 2018). She was an exchange student at National Taiwan University for one semester (2016-2017).

🥈 Transforming Complex Ideas into Accessible Knowledge

With over three years of experience in communications and outreach at the international level, she has supported global outreach and knowledge-sharing initiatives, promoted intellectual property resources to international audiences, and contributed to projects aimed at improving access to patent information and digital services. She specializes in translating complex technical and legal information into clear, engaging, and user-friendly content for diverse global audiences. Her expertise includes newsletter production, social media communications, website content management, and digital campaigns. She also worked with WIPO's Patent Cooperation Treaty (PCT) Legal and User Relations Division on workflow optimization and outreach initiatives.

🥉 Continuously learning on IP - Communication - Project management

Recently, Na Li has expanded her expertise in intellectual property with certificates from WIPO-Harvard Public Health and Patents, WIPO-UNIGE IP Summer School, and various WIPO distance learning courses on patents, copyright, trademarks, and related rights. She also holds certifications in PRINCE2 Project Management and INSEAD Impactful Communication for Leaders. She is skilled in data visualization (Flourish, Power BI, Tableau), digital content creation, and video editing (CapCut, Adobe Premiere Pro).

An enthusiastic learner of emerging technologies, she continuously explores new AI tools and is currently developing her skills in AI-assisted development and vibecoding.
